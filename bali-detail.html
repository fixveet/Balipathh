Premium Balipath Experience

5 Days, 4 Nights
Ubud & Seminyak
4.9 (124 reviews)

Embark on a journey through the heart of Bali. This curated premium experience blends luxury private villa accommodation with authentic cultural deep-dives. From the sacred rice terraces of Ubud to the vibrant beach clubs of Seminyak, every moment is crafted for the discerning traveler.

Luxury Private Villa

Stay in an award-winning infinity pool villa overlooking the river valley.

Cultural Immersion

Private blessings at Tirta Empul and guided tours of local artisan workshops.

Gourmet Dining

Daily chef-prepared breakfast and a 5-course romantic dinner.

Seamless Logistics

VIP airport fast-track and private chauffeur throughout your stay.

Day 1: Arrival & Rejuvenation

VIP welcome at Ngurah Rai International Airport. Transfer to your Ubud villa for a 90-minute traditional Balinese massage and sunset cocktails.

Day 2: Sacred Hearts & Rice Terraces

Early morning visit to Tegalalang Rice Terrace followed by a purification ritual at Tirta Empul Temple.

Day 3: Ubud Artistry to Seminyak Sands

Explore Ubud Art Market, then transfer to your beachfront suite in Seminyak for a sunset beach dinner.

Day 4: Beach & Spa Day

Full-day at leisure — enjoy the private pool, book a spa treatment, or take a surf lesson at Kuta Beach.

Day 5: Farewell Sunrise & Departure

Sunrise breakfast with panoramic views, followed by private transfer to the airport for your onward journey.
